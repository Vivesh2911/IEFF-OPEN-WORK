Program Highlights

Kerala Massage : Rejuvenate yourself with Massage from Kerala Ayurveda center, Get natural with god’s own country Kerala’s massage.

Traditional Dress Tryouts : Saree for Ladies: Draping a Saree with Indian Jewellery & Visits to Temples, which is a gift of pure Hindu tradition.

Kurta & Pyjama for Men : Wearing the Indian attire will make you look more handsome and ethnic.

Mehendi : Mehendi painting with intricate designs, a holy ritual of Indian wedding.

Indian Cooking : Diverse, delicious and not as difficult to cook, understand the basics of Indian food and cooking. Sensing the Aromas & Tasting the flavors of Indian spices. North Indian Roti & Dal - Paneer , South Indian Dosa & Idly.

Street Food : Tasting Mouth watering; lip smacking Indian Street Foods that will make you run for a Bite.

Shopping : Indian Mall & Street Shopping.

Markets : Karol Bagh Market , Sarojin Nagar , Lajpatnagar Market & Chandini Chowk Market

Cultural Workshops : Indian Folk & Classical Dane Forms

Indian Cultural Dance workshops : Punjabi Folk , Rajasthani Folk , Gujarati Folk & Classical Dance Forms
