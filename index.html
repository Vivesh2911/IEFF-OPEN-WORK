    PARTICIPATION

    Festival Terms & Conditions

    • Festival has two participation Categories
      1. Festival Participant (Mandatory)
      2. Festival Competitor (Optional)
    • Festival is open to all dance and music groups of any style: Folk Dances (More preferrable), Theatres, Classical, Contemporary, Latin, Ball room, Street, Modern Social, Oriental, Fusion Dances, Choirs, Brass Bands, Orchestras & majorettes.
    • Age group of the Participants should be 7 - 75+ years.
    • Number of Participants accepted are from Solos to Groups(unlimited).
    • Participants are allowed to perform on live or recorded music.
    • Participants should cover the expense of their International flight for the round-trip along with the travel insurance.
